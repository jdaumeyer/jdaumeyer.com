Landlord-Tenant Communication Tool

  •    2025-12-06
  • work

Developed in collaboration with JusticeTech at the Moritz College of Law, with a cross discipline team. This tool beutifies the language used by tenants when communicating issues to their landlords, in order to prevent angry messages or errors of speech.

I was responsible for the frontend design of the app, ensuring that the form flowed nicely and the site was accessible.
