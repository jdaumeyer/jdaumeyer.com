Colony Cats Website Redesign

  • 2025-04-21
  • work

Colony Cats is a Columbus based cage free cat rescue in need of a refresed website. I worked with a team to provide a redesign of their website. We worked together in Figma to create an interactive mockup which demonstrated our improved functionality. To guide our redesign, we created personas of each different type of user to make sure we accomadeted them in the final design.
